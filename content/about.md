My name is Kuba. Born in 1990.

I'm a tattooer at Poland (Toruń). I am also a die-hard Linux fan who is guided by the beautiful idea of ​​code and software freedom.

My adventure with computers began in the late 90s, when I got (for good grades at school) my very first computer. I went into html/css very soon, starting with creating my first elementary school website. I entered the world of Linux very quickly (mainy thanks to my dad, who started using in in early 90s), which has been my primary operating system for many years. Linux provides incredible fun, control over what you do, and vast possibilities that no other operating system has ever offered me. Unfortunately, I stopped creating for many years, I feel that I wasted my time that I could have devoted to developing programming.

In 2013, after graduating with a degree in philosophy, I started tattooing as an amateur, and then it grew to such proportions that I have been doing it for a living for the last 10 years.

Unfortunately, as with most freelance industries, there are ups and downs. Being a husband and father of three children forced me to change my approach to life and pursue a different career.

So here is my journey.
